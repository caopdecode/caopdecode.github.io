About me

I am a passionate data analyst with a solid background in Multimedia Engineering and a Google Data
Analytics certification. Over the past four years, I have gained experience in data analysis using
tools such as Python, R, SQL, Excel, and Tableau.

My focus is on transforming data into actionable
insights that drive strategic decision-making and continuous process improvement.

In addition to my technical skills, I consider myself a self-taught, committed, and collaborative
individual, always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies and challenges.

I am motivated by
the opportunity to make a positive impact on organizations by helping them optimize processes and
achieve their goals through data. I firmly believe that data analysis is key to the future, and I am
excited to be part of that transformation.
