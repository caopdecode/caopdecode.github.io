CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION

Project

Overview

The Customer Segmentation Project was designed to help the fictional company UK MERCH make better data-driven decisions. The company had been facing challenges due to inefficient resource allocation and a lack of focus on the right customer groups.

By analyzing their sales records from the past year, I implemented RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value) and clustering techniques to segment their customers effectively.

Using tools like Python and libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, I uncovered actionable insights that could help UK MERCH improve their marketing strategies, allocate resources more efficiently, and ultimately increase customer satisfaction and revenue.

This project highlights the power of data analytics in transforming business strategies and demonstrates how advanced segmentation can lead to smarter decisions.
